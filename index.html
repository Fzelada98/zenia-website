What types of companies does ZENIA work with?

We work with growth-stage SMBs across fitness, wellness, retail, ecommerce, and professional services. Our sweet spot is companies with 5-200 employees looking to automate operations and scale without proportionally growing headcount.

How long does it take to see results from AI automation?

Most clients see measurable impact within 4-8 weeks. We start with a strategy session, design the system in 1-2 weeks, and deploy within 4-6 weeks. Continuous optimization begins immediately after launch.

Not at all. We handle everything from strategy to implementation. We immerse ourselves in your operations, understand your workflows, and build systems that your team can use without technical expertise.

We serve companies across Europe and the Americas. Our team works remotely and supports clients globally with bilingual (English/Spanish) service.
