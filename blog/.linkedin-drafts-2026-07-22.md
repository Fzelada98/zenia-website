Sports medicine clinics see 22% of first visits vanish before the athlete ever walks in. That's roughly €150 to €300 of first-visit revenue gone per no-show, before the 4-to-6 rehab sessions that also disappear with them.

The pattern is boring: the athlete feels a little better on Tuesday, they don't confirm the Thursday slot, and by Friday they're at the clinic two streets over that answered their WhatsApp in 10 seconds.

We put an AI agent on that WhatsApp inbox. Triage before first visit, 48h + 3h + 20min reminder chain, pain 0-10 check-in after each rehab session with automatic escalation to the physio if the patient reports an 8. In pilot clinics no-show on first visits drops from 22% to around 6%, and rehab block dropout goes from 32% to 14%.

The interesting number isn't the no-show. It's the 38% of appointment requests that land outside reception hours: Saturday 07:15, Tuesday 22:40. Manual triage sends those to the competitor by Monday morning.
