## DRAFT score=12 — agente-ia-para-academias-de-pintura

A painting academy with 120 active students loses 6 enrollments a month because nobody answers WhatsApp on Sunday night, forgets tomorrow's class reminder, or double-books the watercolor advanced slot.

That is roughly 510 EUR of direct revenue plus another 470 EUR in replacement lead cost (meta ads sit around 40 EUR per qualified art lead in Spain). Nine thousand a year quietly walking to the studio around the corner.

We deployed a personalized AI agent trained on the real workshop catalog: oil, watercolor, pastel, illustration, levels, teacher rotations. It replies in 8 seconds 24/7, closes trial-class bookings before the aspirant finds a competitor, sends the 18-hour class confirmation and pings students who miss two weeks with a make-up offer before they churn.

Across 18 art academies at month 3: trial to enrollment conversion 34% to 52%, monthly churn 7.4% to 4.1%, admin hours 14 to 3.5 per week, 38% of new inquiries now closed outside opening hours.
