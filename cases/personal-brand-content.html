Personal Brand United States Content Creation
The Challenge

$750/Month for 8 Pieces of Content

This creator had built a strong personal brand but was struggling with content production. Their agency was delivering the bare minimum, and scaling output meant scaling costs linearly.

The situation:

  • $750/month to a content agency for 8 total pieces (mix of short-form and long-form)
  • Generic output that didn't fully capture the creator's voice, style, or expertise
  • Slow turnaround: 5-7 business days per batch, no flexibility for trending topics
  • No scalability: doubling output meant doubling the budget to $1,500/month
  • Missed opportunities: the brand needed consistent daily presence but could only afford biweekly drops

The creator knew content volume was the growth lever, but the economics of traditional production didn't work.

The Solution

An AI Content Engine with Avatar & Voice Clone

We designed and deployed a full AI-powered content production system. This isn't a template generator. It's a complete production pipeline that creates on-brand content at scale.

The system includes:

  • AI avatar: a digital version of the creator that appears in short-form videos, maintaining visual brand consistency without requiring the creator to be on camera for every piece
  • Voice cloning: trained on the creator's actual voice patterns, tone, and speech cadence for narration and audio content
  • Content intelligence: AI that understands the creator's niche, audience interests, and engagement patterns to suggest topics and angles
  • Multi-format output: the same core idea gets adapted into shorts, long-form pieces, captions, and scripts automatically
  • Brand voice engine: every piece sounds like the creator wrote it, because the AI was trained on their existing body of work

The creator still provides strategic direction and reviews output. But the heavy lifting of production, editing, formatting, and scheduling is fully automated.
