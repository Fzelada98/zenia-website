Subject: Expert source: Lessons learned deploying AI agents for SMB operations

Building multi-agent workflows for SMBs reveals failure modes absent from enterprise case studies. Chief among them: orchestration overhead that erases ROI for small teams when coordination complexity isn't managed early.

At Zenia Partners, we have deployed agentic AI systems for 15+ Latin American SMBs across customer retention, WhatsApp CRM, and operational workflows. The core lesson: orchestration complexity scales non-linearly. A single AI agent adds value; three unchecked agents in a workflow produce contradictory outputs and customer-facing errors within days. Human-in-the-loop checkpoints are not optional at this scale. They are the difference between 40% churn reduction and a complete rollback.

One F&B client (Freshly Baked) saw a 28% improvement in customer reactivation rates once we shifted from fully autonomous multi-agent flows to a hybrid model where agents handle scoring and message drafting, but humans approve segment-level decisions before send.

Fabrizzio Zelada, Founder of Zenia Partners (zeniapartners.com)
